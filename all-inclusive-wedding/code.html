Frequently Asked Questions

Do my guests have to stay at the resort?
Yes, to qualify for wedding packages and ensure a seamless experience for your group, we require guests to stay on property. Non-hotel guests may attend the ceremony with a purchased day pass.
Can you handle dietary restrictions?
Absolutely. Our culinary teams are experienced in managing allergies, vegan, gluten-free, and kosher-style preferences for your private wedding events.
What about entertainment?
Basic audio for ceremonies is included. We also offer upgrades for DJs, live mariachi bands, fire dancers, and more to make your reception unforgettable.
