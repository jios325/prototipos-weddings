Good to Know

description

Permits & Legalities

Civil ceremonies in Mexico require blood tests and witnesses. Our planners handle all documentation to ensure your union is legally binding.

calendar_month

Best Months

November through April offers the most pleasant weather with lower humidity. May and June offer great value before the warmer summer months.

air

Wind Tips

Ocean breezes are refreshing but can be strong. We recommend updos for brides and secure decor elements like weighted vases.

umbrella

Backup Plans

Nature can be unpredictable. All our beach venues have stunning indoor ballrooms or covered terraces reserved as a Plan B.
