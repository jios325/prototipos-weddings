5 Expert Ways to Save More

Maximize your budget with these insider tips from our wedding planners.

1

Choose "Shoulder Season"

Booking your date in May, June, September, or October can lower travel and accommodation costs by up to 30% compared to peak winter months.

2

Mid-Week Celebrations

Venues often offer reduced rates or added perks for weddings held on Tuesdays, Wednesdays, or Thursdays.

3

Leverage Group Benefits

With our group booking policies, every room your guests book earns you credits toward your wedding package, sometimes making the ceremony free.

4

Stick to Local Flowers

Imported blooms cost extra. Tropical flowers like orchids and lilies are abundant locally and just as stunning for a fraction of the price.

5

Book Early

Locking in your date 12-18 months in advance secures current pricing before annual inflation adjustments.
